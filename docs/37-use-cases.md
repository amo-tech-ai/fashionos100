# Real-World Use Cases

## 1. The Full-Service Brand Launch
**Client:** A new sustainable shoe brand.
*   **Step 1:** User runs **Brand Wizard** (URL) to establish DNA.
*   **Step 2:** Books **Photography** (Ecom + Lifestyle) via Wizard.
*   **Step 3:** Books **Web Design**. AI auto-populates the site with photos from Step 2.
*   **Step 4:** **Social Wizard** generates a 30-day launch calendar using the assets.

## 2. The Corporate Summit
**Client:** Tech company hosting a conference.
*   **Step 1:** **Event Wizard** scouts venues in San Francisco via Maps.
*   **Step 2:** AI builds a budget and schedule.
*   **Step 3:** **Video Team** booked to record keynotes.
*   **Step 4:** **Sponsor CRM** identifies and scores potential partners.

## 3. The Restaurant Pivot
**Client:** A bistro launching delivery.
*   **Step 1:** **eCommerce Wizard** sets up a menu store.
*   **Step 2:** **Photo Wizard** guides a food shoot.
*   **Step 3:** **Marketing Wizard** runs local ads targeting a 5-mile radius (Maps Grounding).

## 4. NYFW Runway Show
**Client:** High-fashion designer.
*   **Step 1:** **Event Wizard** manages the 14-stage timeline.
*   **Step 2:** **Casting** manages 30 models.
*   **Step 3:** **Veo 3.1** generates a teaser video from the show theme description.
*   **Step 4:** **ROI Dashboard** tracks press mentions and buyer orders.
