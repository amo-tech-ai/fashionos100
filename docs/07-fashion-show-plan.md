
# ‚≠ê **Complete Fashion Event Planning Guide ‚Äî With Accessories Added**

---

# **PHASE 1 ‚Äî Concept & Creative Vision**

### **1. Event Purpose**

Stakeholders: **Designer, Producer**

* Launch new collection
* Showcase accessories line
* Pop-up, runway, exhibition, trunk show

### **2. Creative Direction**

Stakeholders: **Designer, Stylist, Accessories Lead**

* Moodboard
* Color palette
* Fabrics + materials
* Accessories styling (bags, shoes, hats, jewelry) integrated into the story

---

# **PHASE 2 ‚Äî Designer, Dresses, and Accessories Prep**

### **3. Collection Development**

Stakeholders: **Designer + Tailors + Accessories Team**

* Finalize garments
* Finalize accessories:
  * Bags
  * Shoes
  * Scarves
  * Hats
  * Eyewear
  * Belts
  * Jewelry
  * Gloves
* Create styling combinations (look-by-look pairing)
* Accessories board per look
* Label and inventory accessories
* Backup sets for accessories

**Real-world note:**
Accessories are often 50% of the final runway look ‚Äî they shape silhouette, identity, and luxury feel.

---

# **PHASE 3 ‚Äî Model Casting + Fittings + Accessories Testing**

### **4. Casting Models**

Stakeholders: **Model Agency, Designer, Stylist**

* Select models based on:
  * Height
  * Walk
  * Look
  * Fit with accessories (e.g., shoe size, jewelry fit)

### **5. Fittings**

Stakeholders: **Designer, Tailor, Accessories Lead**

* Test garments on models
* Match accessories to each outfit
* Adjust:
  * Shoe sizes
  * Jewelry length
  * Belts fit
  * Hats/headpieces fit
* Photograph final looks
* Create lineup = outfit + accessories + hair/makeup inspiration

---

# **PHASE 4 ‚Äî Venue, Runway, Lighting, Setup**

### **6. Venue Selection**

Stakeholders: **Organizer, Venue Manager**

* Space for accessories tables backstage
* Runway lighting designed to highlight jewelry/fabrics

### **7. Runway Production**

Stakeholders: **Lighting Crew, Stage Designer**

* Lighting angles to make accessories sparkle
* Camera-friendly lighting for jewelry
* Sturdy runway for footwear stability

---

# **PHASE 5 ‚Äî Sponsors, Partnerships, Accessories Labels**

### **8. Sponsor Integrations**

Stakeholders: **Brand Partners, Sponsors**

* Accessories brand collaborations (jewelry, handbags, eyewear)
* Placement in lookbook & event visuals
* Sponsor booths / exhibitions

---

# **PHASE 6 ‚Äî Photography, Videography & Content**

### **9. Content Capture**

Stakeholders: **Photographers, Videographers**
Capture:

* Close-up shots of accessories
* Behind-the-scenes dressing of accessories
* Runway details (hands, earrings, shoes)
* Macro jewelry shots
* Product photography for marketing

---

# **PHASE 7 ‚Äî Backstage & Show Execution**

### **10. Backstage Accessories Zone**

Stakeholders: **Accessories Team, Backstage Crew**
Create an accessories station:

* Labeled trays
* Anti-tarnish cloths
* Shoe racks
* Jewelry stands
* Mirrors + lighting for quick checks
* Accessories checklist per model
* Accessories dresser assigned to models

### **11. Run-of-Show Management**

Stakeholders: **Stage Manager, Designer, Accessories Lead**

* Accessories ready 15‚Äì20 minutes before each show
* Quick-change accessories team
* Final walk-through for:
  * Bag straps
  * Jewelry clasps
  * Shoelaces
  * Hat stability

### **12. Show Execution**

Stakeholders: **All Teams**

* Dressers assist with accessories
* Model check at lineup:
  * Earrings secured
  * Bracelets fastened
  * Bags positioned
  * Shoes checked

---

# **PHASE 8 ‚Äî Guest Experience & Ticketing**

### **13. Front-of-House**

Stakeholders: **Ushers & PR**

* Display accessories in VIP lounge
* Sponsor accessory booth
* Photo wall featuring accessories

---

# **PHASE 9 ‚Äî Post-Show Operations**

### **14. Accessories Return & Inventory**

Stakeholders: **Accessories Coordinator**

* Collect every piece
* Inspect for damage
* Return borrowed items
* Document usage for PR press kits

---

# **PHASE 10 ‚Äî Marketing & PR**

### **15. Press & Social Posts**

Stakeholders: **PR, Social Media Manager**
Include:

* Close-ups of accessories
* Designer commentary on accessories
* Reels/TikTok: ‚ÄúAll the accessories from the show‚Äù
* Still-life photography for after-show promotions

---

# **PHASE 11 ‚Äî Final Reporting & Analytics**

### **16. Event Recap**

Stakeholders: **Organizer, Designer**

* Photos + videos
* Social reach
* Accessories-focused coverage
* Sponsor deliverables
* What worked + what to improve

## 3Ô∏è‚É£ React Component List (Mobile Fashion Show Planner)

Think in layers:

* **App shell & routing**
* **Global UI (navigation, layout, sheets)**
* **Screen components**
* **Shared building blocks (cards, lists, forms)**

### A. App Shell & Navigation

* `<AppRoot />`

  * Initializes Supabase client, auth, query client, theme.
  * Handles ‚Äúlogged in / logged out‚Äù gating.

* `<MobileLayout />`

  * Wraps all screens with:

    * Safe area
    * Header slot
    * Bottom navigation

* `<BottomNav />`

  * Tabs: Home, Timeline, Tasks, Stakeholders, More.
  * Props:

    * `currentTab`
    * `onTabChange(tabKey)`

* (Optional) `<RouteManager />` or router config

  * Defines routes for each screen component.

---

### B. Global UI & Utilities

* `<HeaderBar />`

  * Props: `title`, `subtitle?`, `primaryAction?`, `onBack?`.

* `<ScreenContainer />`

  * Standard padding, background, vertical scroll.

* `<FloatingActionButton />`

## 4Ô∏è‚É£ Screen ‚Üí Supabase Tables Mapping

Here‚Äôs how each screen maps to **‚Äúread / write‚Äù** on your Supabase schema.

I‚Äôll reference the tables you‚Äôve defined:

* Core: `users`, `events`, `event_phases`, `tasks`, `task_assignees`, `stakeholders`, `stakeholder_roles`, `event_stakeholders`, `notes`/`activity_log`
* Fashion entities:
  `venues`, `sponsor_organizations`, `event_sponsors`,
  `organizer_teams`, `organizer_team_members`,
  `fashion_brands`, `designer_profiles`, `event_designers`,
  `model_agencies`, `model_profiles`, `event_models`
* Availability / scheduling:
  `venue_availability`, `model_availability`, `designer_availability`,
  `event_schedule`, `event_rehearsals`, `call_times`, `backstage_items`
* Analytics: views like `event_overview_view`, `task_with_assignees_view`, etc. (optional but recommended)

---

### 1. Home / Dashboard (`<HomeScreen />`)

**Reads from:**

* `events`

  * Current event summary (status, date, location).
* `event_phases`

  * To compute progress per section (Vision, Casting, etc.).
* `tasks` + `task_assignees`

  * For ‚Äúopen tasks‚Äù / quick stats.
* Optional analytics views:

  * `event_overview_view` (pre-joined KPIs).

**Writes to:**

* `events`

  * When user taps ‚Äú+ New Event‚Äù and creates one.

---

### 2. Event Detail (`<EventDetailScreen />`)

**Reads from:**

* `events`
* `event_phases` (phase counts / statuses)
* `tasks` (counts by status)
* `event_designers`, `event_models`, `event_sponsors`
* `venues` (via `events.venue_id`)

**Writes to:**

* `events`

  * Update event metadata (status, date, etc.).
* Maybe `notes`

  * Event-level notes / comments.

---

### 3. Timeline (`<TimelineScreen />`)

**Reads from:**

* `event_phases`
* `events` (for context: name, date)
* `event_stakeholders`, `stakeholders`

  * To show assignee avatars per phase.

**Writes to:**

* `event_phases`

  * Update `status`, `due_date`, maybe `description`.
* (Optional) `notes`

  * If you log comments per phase.

---

### 4. Tasks (`<TasksScreen />`)

**Reads from:**

* `tasks`
* `task_assignees`
* `event_phases` (link tasks to phases)
* `stakeholders` (to show assignees)
* Filters by:

  * `event_id`, `phase_id`, `status`, `due_date`.

**Writes to:**

* `tasks`

  * Create / update / change status, due date, priority, description.
* `task_assignees`

  * Adding / removing assignees.
* `notes` / `activity_log`

  * When task status changes (optional).

---

### 5. Stakeholders Hub (`<StakeholdersScreen />`, list & detail)

**Reads from:**

* `stakeholder_roles`

  * To display role types.
* `stakeholders`
* `event_stakeholders`

  * Filter stakeholders for the current event.

**Writes to:**

* `stakeholders`

  * Create / update basic person/org info.
* `event_stakeholders`

  * Attach/detach stakeholders to/from an event.

---

### 6. Venues & Availability (`<VenuesScreen />`, `<VenueDetailScreen />`)

**Reads from:**

* `venues`
* `events`

  * Show which events are tied to each venue (via `events.venue_id`).
* `venue_availability`

  * Time/slot-based availability.

**Writes to:**

* `venues`

  * Add/edit venue info.
* `events`

  * Assign `venue_id` to event.
* `venue_availability`

  * Mark available/blocked/reserved slots.

---

### 7. Models & Casting (`<ModelsCastingScreen />`, `<ModelDetailScreen />`)

* `model_agencies`

**Writes to:**

* `model_profiles`

  * Link models to events, set `look_count`, `is_opening`, `is_closing`.
* `model_availability`

  * Fittings, rehearsals, show-time availability.

  * Meeting, fitting, rehearsal availability.

  * Sponsor-related notes.

* `event_schedule`

  * Add/edit rehearsals.

**Reads from (mostly views):**

* `tasks`

  * `task_stats_view`
  * `sponsor_value_view`

### 13. AI Copilot (`<AIcopilotSheet />`)

**Reads from:**

* Supabase tables indirectly (through Edge Functions), e.g. `events`, `event_phases`, `tasks`, `stakeholders`, `availability` tables.

* `tasks`

  * AI-generated notes (run-of-show, PR draft).

**Implementation note:**

* Frontend calls a Supabase **Edge Function**, which calls Gemini and returns structured suggestions.
* UI then maps those suggestions ‚Üí `INSERT` / `UPDATE` calls.
