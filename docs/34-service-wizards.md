# AI Service Wizards

## 1. Photography Wizard
*   **Inputs:** Product type, Style (Editorial/Ecom), Quantity.
*   **AI:** Generates a **Shotlist** based on the products. Suggests "Props" and "Lighting".

## 2. Video Wizard
*   **Inputs:** Platform (TikTok/TV), Duration, Message.
*   **AI:** Writes a **Script** and generates a **Storyboard** description.

## 3. Web Design Wizard
*   **Inputs:** Industry, Vibe, Features (Shop, Blog).
*   **AI:** Generates a **Sitemap** and **Wireframe** structure. Writes initial homepage copy.

## 4. eCommerce Wizard
*   **Inputs:** Product list (CSV/URL).
*   **AI:** Maps products to categories. Generates **SEO Descriptions** and **Meta Tags**.

## 5. Social Media Wizard
*   **Inputs:** Campaign goal, Date range.
*   **AI:** Creates a **Content Calendar** with post ideas, caption drafts, and hashtag sets.

## 6. Marketing Wizard
*   **Inputs:** Target audience, Offer.
*   **AI:** Designs a **Marketing Funnel** (Ad -> Landing Page -> Email). Drafts ad copy.
